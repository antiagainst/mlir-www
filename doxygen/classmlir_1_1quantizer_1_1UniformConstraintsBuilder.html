Propagates an explicit scale from an anchor that may have a uniform |selectedType| to the |explicitScaleZeroPoint| field of the to node.

This is typically used with a to node that has a candidate quantized type of |UniformExplicitFixedPointScale|, indicating that it can be an arbitrary (signed) type that is expected to share the same scale as the originating node.
